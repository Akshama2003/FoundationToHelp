FAQ Section

What is Sujok Therapy?
Sujok therapy is a holistic healing system developed by Professor Park Jae Woo . It treats illnesses by stimulating corresponding points on the hands and feet. The therapy is based on the principle that each part of the body is represented by micro-points on the hands and feet, similar to reflexology
1. How Does Sujok Therapy Work?
Sujok therapy works by stimulating these micro-points on the hands and feet to treat diseases in other parts of the body . It can be used for preventive care by strengthening the immune system and for treating various physical, mental, and emotional health issues
2. Is There Formal Training Available for Sujok Therapy?
Yes, formal training is available for Sujok therapy. There are courses offered at different levels, typically ranging from beginner to advanced . These courses often include hands-on training and may lead to certification upon completion.
3. Can Sujok Therapy Be Learned at Home?
While formal courses exist, it's possible to start learning about Sujok therapy at home. Some online resources and low-budget courses are available for those interested in self-study. However, for professional practice, formal training is generally recommended.
4. What Kind of Health Issues Can Sujok Therapy Address?
Sujok therapy can be used to address a wide range of health issues, including:
- Common ailments like headaches and stress
- Chronic conditions
- Physical health problems
- Mental and emotional health issues
It's important to note that while Sujok therapy can be beneficial, it should not replace conventional medical treatment. It's best used as a complementary therapy under the guidance of a qualified practitioner.
